About Aetheris

We source 100% authentic fragrances and deliver them with care. Since 2020, we've helped thousands find their perfect scent.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by passionate fragrance enthusiasts, Aetheris was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves to find their signature scent. What started as a small boutique specializing in rare and authentic perfumes has grown into a trusted destination for fragrance lovers worldwide.

Our team of expert perfumers and curators personally test every fragrance in our collection, ensuring that each bottle meets our uncompromising standards for quality and authenticity. We believe that a great perfume should tell a story, evoke memories, and become an integral part of your identity.

Our Mission

To democratize luxury fragrances by making authentic, high-quality perfumes accessible to everyone, while providing exceptional service and expertise to help customers discover their perfect scent.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted fragrance destination, known for our authenticity, expertise, and commitment to helping every customer find their signature scent that truly represents who they are.
