Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your fragrance. If you're not completely satisfied, we're here to help.

30-Day Return Policy

We offer a full 30-day return window for unopened products. Your satisfaction is our priority!

Return Policy

  • 30-day return window from purchase date
  • Products must be unopened and in original packaging
  • Original receipt or order confirmation required
  • Free return shipping for defective items
  • Refunds processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refund issued to original payment method

Exchange Policy

  • Free exchanges for different fragrances
  • Size exchanges available (subject to availability)
  • Exchange requests within 14 days of delivery
  • Original packaging must be intact
  • Price difference applies for upgrades
  • Exchanges shipped within 2-3 business days

How to Return

  1. Contact our customer service team
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Receive return authorization and shipping label
  4. Package items securely in original packaging
  5. Ship using provided return label
  6. Track your return status online

Non-Returnable Items

  • Opened or used products
  • Items damaged by customer
  • Products purchased more than 30 days ago
  • Custom or personalized items
  • Gift cards and digital products
  • Items without original packaging
