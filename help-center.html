Help Center

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, returns, and more.

How do I place an order? +

To place an order, simply browse our collection, select your desired fragrance, and click "Add to Cart". Proceed to checkout, enter your shipping information, and complete your payment. You'll receive a confirmation email once your order is processed.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

How long does shipping take? +

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Express shipping (1-2 business days) and overnight shipping are also available. International shipping times vary by location.

Can I return or exchange a product? +

Yes! We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or exchange. Please visit our Returns page for detailed instructions.

Are your products authentic? +

Absolutely! We guarantee 100% authenticity for all our fragrances. We source directly from authorized distributors and manufacturers. Every product comes with a certificate of authenticity.

How do I track my order? +

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the carrier's website. You can also check your order status in your account dashboard.
